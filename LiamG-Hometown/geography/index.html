You won’t have to worry about ridiculous dips and dives on the roads! The landscape in Scandia is almost entirely flat ground, making it a perfect spot for all kinds of structures. Almost all the roads are paved, giving everyone a smooth driving experience. Anyone can easily walk across town; it’ll take most people 15-20 minutes from one end of town to the other. You won’t have to worry about getting lost here! The town’s main roads are a triangular shape, which connects to roads within the triangle.

On our website, you can find out everything you need to know about our little community! Enjoy your visit!
