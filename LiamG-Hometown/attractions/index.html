Come visit Scandia’s very own EID Historical Park, which holds several buildings from Scandia’s past! Buildings like the blacksmith’s barn, the grocery store, and of course; the grain elevator. The museum grounds are supported consistently, but a lot of the old items sitting outside are still rotting away. Leaving the items as they were makes sure the history of these items isn’t tainted in any way. You won’t be seeing many out-of-place or low-quality restorations out here!

On our website, you can find out everything you need to know about our little community! Enjoy your visit!
