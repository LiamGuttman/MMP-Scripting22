The residents of Scandia are incredibly close with each other. Even though they’ve got a wide range of people from all over North America, they’re all extremely friendly! They get together for events every few months, and usually keep in touch through their very own Facebook community! It might only be a community of around 170 people, but even the newest residents will be treated like they’ve been here for years! Every year they celebrate Earl Taylor Day, where the museum gets “transported” back to the past. You can visit the blacksmith, the grocery store, and even listen to some live music.

Agriculture is a big part of Scandia’s economy, with tons of farmland surrounding the hamlet. Just outside of town, ICI (Independent Crop Inputs) has built their main service center, where they supply a wide range of products and services for local farmers. There’s also Scandia Honey, situated in one of the corners of town. They produce and sell everything from honey to wax. The honey company has been running in the town for over 250 years, making them a serious piece of local history!

On our website, you can find out everything you need to know about our little community! Enjoy your visit!
