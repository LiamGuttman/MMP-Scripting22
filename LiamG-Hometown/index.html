If you’ve ever wanted to visit a quiet town with a great community, Scandia is a fantastic place to visit! It might not be the biggest or most well-known spot in Southern Alberta, but that makes it even easier to explore every little section. There’s not much out here to miss, but you’ll love what’s here. They’ve got a museum, a dining hall, playground, and even a little coffee shop. It’s perfect for a quick little visit if you’re ever out exploring around in the Brooks area!
