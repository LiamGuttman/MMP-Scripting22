Learning Reflection

  • What moments were you most proud of your efforts in this course? Why?

    • I was really proud of my work on the Hometown Assignment. I got to put together this sort of "time capsule" of my real hometown, and it was really fun. I used the skills I had started building with previous assignments and tried to crank that up to 11. I felt like the color scheme was a great choice.

  • Provide an example of a tough situation or barrier you handled well in this course and the key strengths you relied on.

    • Learning JavaScript was a bit of a hard learning curve. I was super sick during the week we really started digging into it, so it was tough to get a good grasp on learning it. I've finally started understanding it, but it's probably going to take a bit longer to really get used to it.

  • If you could do anything in this course over again, what, if anything, would you change?

    • I would probably go back and add more detail into my Mad Libs page. I was running out of time and totally rushed it. I am not afraid to admit that. At least it functioned like it was supposed to. That's probably the best part.

  • What actions/behaviours should you start doing that would help you in future courses?

    • I should probably have a bookmark directly to w3schools, instead of having to type or search it. It's extremely useful and I don't know why I haven't done this yet. I also need to look into finding extra sources for learning code stuff.
